ABOUT


I AM EVA

A front-end web developer based in New York City.


WHY

Although I have a background in social work I've been a life-long artist and wanted to make a career change to something more creative. Making websites looked fun so I taught myself HTML and CSS. I knew I needed to level up and finally learn to program so I took a front-end web development course at General Assembly. I improved my HTML and CSS a bunch and finally conquered my fear of coding and got into Javascript. I'm still learning (and hope to always be) but I'm having a lot of fun along the way!


When I'm not developing I work with the mentally ill. I run computer workshops at work as well and love teaching and learning from coworkers and clients. I'm looking to develop full time eventually and think my social work background gives me great people skills to go with my technical skills.


IN MY FREE TIME

You can find me at various cafes in the lower east side trying to look mysterious by typing frantically into the terminal (I'm probably just pushing to github. Got to keep my streak up.), second-hand shopping or getting challeged to tennis matches by children in the park (he won).
